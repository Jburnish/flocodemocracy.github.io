About Us

Building a stronger Florence County — together.

FloCo Democracy is a year-round organizing hub for Florence County, South Carolina Democrats. We provide the tools, training, and community volunteers need to make a real difference — whether you're knocking doors for the first time or a seasoned campaign veteran.

Our Microsoft Bookings system makes it easy to reserve a shift that fits your schedule for canvassing, phone banking, and social media training — no experience required.
