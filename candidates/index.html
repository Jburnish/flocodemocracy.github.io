Jane Doe

Florence County Council, District 1

Jane Doe has been a Florence County resident for over 20 years, raising her family and building her small business right here in our community. She believes in transparent, accountable government that works for every resident.

John Smith

SC House of Representatives, District 57

John Smith grew up in Florence and attended Florence-Darlington Technical College before serving our country in the United States Army for eight years. He returned home with a deep commitment to public service.

John's platform centers on fully funding public education, protecting voting rights, and ensuring that every veteran in the Pee Dee region receives the benefits and care they have earned.
